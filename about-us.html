ABOUT COMPANY

We Are Best In Architect Company In US.

BuildCrest Construction Corp is a California-based General Engineering Contractor (CSLB Class A) specializing in construction and infrastructure projects of all sizes. We deliver technically demanding public works and site development projects with a strong commitment to safety, quality, and performance.

Our leadership team brings over a decade of experience delivering infrastructure and construction projects for the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), the U.S. military, cities, Caltrans, and other government clients. These projects required strict compliance with U.S. Government standards, detailed quality control systems, disciplined project management, and comprehensive safety programs.

That same level of accountability, structure, and operational excellence now defines BuildCrest Construction Corp.

Projects / Leadership Experience

While BuildCrest Construction Corp is newly established in California, our leadership team brings over a decade of experience delivering complex infrastructure and public works projects for U.S. Government and institutional clients, operating under strict quality control, safety, and compliance standards.

Representative Projects

  • U.S. Army Corps of Engineers – Training & Administrative Facility

    Two-story masonry facility including offices, support areas, fitness and community spaces, and a daycare with playground. Delivered under USACE quality, safety, and compliance standards.

  • U.S. Army Corps of Engineers – Daycare & Billet Facility

    Construction of a two-story masonry facility including a daycare, billet accommodations, and playground. Scope included site preparation and improvements, water and sanitary systems, electrical distribution, and geotechnical/design support. Delivered under USACE quality, safety, and compliance standards.

  • U.S. Army Corps of Engineers – Camp Infrastructure Upgrades

    Design and construction of a new asphalt road, stormwater drainage improvements, reinforced concrete retaining wall, and a 15-meter free-standing communications tower. Delivered under USACE quality, safety, and compliance standards.

  • U.S. Army Corps of Engineers – Biometric Building Renovation

    Renovation of an administrative building including upgrades to power, electrical, communications, and security systems. Scope included design verification, material, labor, and equipment, delivered under USACE quality, safety, and compliance standards.
